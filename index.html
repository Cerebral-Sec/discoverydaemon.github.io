Discovery Daemon

Discovery Daemon is a workflow engine software, combining continuous asset monitoring and vulnerability assessment to help companies identify security risks in their digital assets against future threats by setting up a pipeline of tools(Agents) and trigger them based on a schedule or events.

Ask Demo

What is it?

It is a platform where you can connect multiple existing security softwares independently of each other, run them continuously in the background in a workflow, and generate alarms according to certain situations by regularly monitoring their output.

Features

Drag and Drop

Discovery Daemon lets you to drag and drop your agents to creat processes

Case Study

Fully Customizable Workflow

Discovery Daemon does not depend or does not have hard coded specific Agents. That makes it flexible for every workflow. You can use your own private tools or you can use public tools to create a unique workflow.

Case Study

Main Mission

Its main task is to monitor all the assets of a company on the internet in real time and to inform both configuration errors and software weaknesses as quickly as possible. However, you can shape it's workflow as you wish to turn it into something else.
